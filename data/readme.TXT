Probation and Parole in the United States, 2007 - Statistical Tables NCJ 224707

 
This zip archive contains tables in individual .csv spreadsheets from
Probation and Parole in the United States, 2007 - Statistical Tables NCJ 224707 
(electronic only). The full electronic report is available at:
http://www.ojp.usdoj.gov/bjs/abstract/ppus07st.htm
 
This report is one in a series.  More recent editions
may be available.  To view a list of all in the series go to
http://www.ojp.usdoj.gov/bjs/pubalp2.htm#Probation
